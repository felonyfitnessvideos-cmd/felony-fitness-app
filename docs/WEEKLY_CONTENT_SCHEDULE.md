- Skip that day's content addition
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Don't try to "catch up" - maintain weekly rhythm
- Adjust next week's plan if needed
- Document any blockers or challenges

## üí° Content Creation Best Practices

### For Meals:
1. Use common, accessible ingredients
2. Keep prep times realistic (15-30 minutes)
3. Provide substitution options
4. Double-check macro calculations
5. Test the meal yourself if possible

### For Programs:
1. Follow progressive overload principles
2. Include deload weeks for 8+ week programs
3. Provide clear progression criteria
4. Consider different equipment access levels
5. Write helpful coaching cues

### For Pro Routines:
1. Optimize exercise order (compounds first)
2. Provide equipment alternatives
3. Define intensity clearly (RPE or % of 1RM)
4. Include warmup and cooldown protocols
5. Add coaching notes per exercise
